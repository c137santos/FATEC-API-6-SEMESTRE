# 📌 [09 - USER STORY] — Report Sharing via Email

> ✍️ **Author:** Paloma Soares &nbsp;|&nbsp; 🗓️ **Edited:** Apr. 12 &nbsp;|&nbsp; 🔖 **Version:** 2.0 &nbsp;|&nbsp; ✅ **Status:** `Ready`

---

## 🎯 1. Overview (Context)

- **Objective:** Allow Tecsys sales consultants to send the PDF report generated by the platform directly to an email address, with the recipient field pre-filled using their own corporate email address and the option to edit the recipient before sending.
- **Problem:** After the PDF is automatically generated, the consultant needs a fast way to take it outside the platform — either to access it on a mobile device before a meeting or to send it directly to the utility company engineer. Without this feature, the workflow would require manually downloading the file and opening another application to send it.
- **Success:** The consultant confirms the email delivery in fewer than 3 clicks, receives visual feedback of success within 5 seconds, and the PDF arrives correctly in the recipient’s inbox as an attachment.

The email field must display a red border and an immediate validation error message if the format is invalid. The "Confirm Send" button must remain disabled while the field is empty or invalid.

## 📋 3. Narrative

**As** a Tecsys sales consultant,
**I want** to send the PDF report generated by the platform directly to an email address of my choice, with my corporate email already suggested,
**So that** I can quickly take the presentation material outside the platform — whether to access it on my phone before a meeting or send it directly to the utility company engineer — without needing to download the file and use another application.

---

### Happy Path

1. The consultant is on the generated report screen and clicks "Send via Email".
2. The modal opens with the recipient field pre-filled using the corporate email extracted from the JWT.
3. The consultant keeps or edits the recipient address.
4. The consultant clicks "Confirm Send".
5. FastAPI sends the email with the PDF attached through SMTP/Amazon SES.
6. The Front-end displays a visual success confirmation within 5 seconds.
7. The recipient receives the email with the PDF attached and the sender address `noreply@tecsys.com.br`.

## 🛡️ 4. Business Rules

* **Data Source:** PostgreSQL database — used to retrieve the default email address of the authenticated consultant through JWT.
* **Fixed Sender:** The email `From` field must always be `noreply@tecsys.com.br`. The email chosen by the consultant is the recipient (`To`), not the sender.
* **Flexible Recipient:** There are no domain restrictions for the recipient address.

### Dependencies

* Directly depends on [US-07 (Automatic PDF Report Generation)](/docs/user-stories/user-story-07.md).
* Depends on [US-08 (Secure Platform Access)](/docs/user-stories/user-story-08.md) for extracting the consultant email from the JWT.
* Requires a transactional email provider configured in the Back-end (e.g., SendGrid or Amazon SES).

### Specific Rules

* The email body must use a standardized HTML template with clear text identifying the Tecsys platform and the referenced utility company.
* The PDF must be sent as an **attachment** (not as a link), ensuring access without requiring a platform account.
* Anti-spam security: the sender domain must have SPF and DKIM properly configured.

## ✅ 5. Acceptance Criteria

### Automatic Recipient Pre-Fill

| Step           | Action|
| -------------- | ----------------- |
| **Given that** | the consultant clicked the "Send via Email" button|
| **When**       | the email modal is displayed|
| **Then**       | the email field must be pre-filled with the corporate email address associated with the logged-in consultant account, extracted through JWT/PostgreSQL |

---

### Recipient Editing and Validation

| Step           | Action|
| -------------- | ----------------- |
| **Given that** | the email field is editable|
| **When**       | the consultant removes the suggested email and enters a new address|
| **Then**       | the system must validate the format in real time and enable the "Confirm Send" button only when the entered email is valid |

---

### Email Delivery and Success Confirmation

| Step           | Action|
| -------------- | ------------------- |
| **Given that** | a valid email address is filled in and the consultant clicked "Confirm Send"|
| **When**       | the Back-end receives the request|
| **Then**       | it must trigger the delivery service, send the PDF as an attachment, and return HTTP 200 OK so the Front-end can display the success notification within 5 seconds |

---

### Email Delivery Failure

| Step           | Action|
| -------------- | -------------- |
| **Given that** | the consultant confirmed the send action|
| **When**       | a failure occurs in the email service (SMTP/SES unavailable)|
| **Then**       | the system must display: *"Unable to send the email. Please try again in a few moments."* and register the error in the internal logs |

---
